The Future of Brand Development

Revolutionizing how brands are conceived, developed and scaled through AI-powered innovation

Founded in November 2024 by Henry Hayward and Christian Ingham, Magno Brands Limited is revolutionizing how brands are conceived, developed and scaled. We're a modern brand development company that leverages AI-powered automation and streamlined processes to build multiple successful brands faster and more efficiently than traditional methods.

Operating from our London headquarters with international teams across Europe and Asia, we've created a unique ecosystem where innovative ideas are rapidly transformed into thriving brands within our diverse portfolio.
